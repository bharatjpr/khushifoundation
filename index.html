About Us

Who We Are?

Khushi Foundation, an NGO came into existence in 2017. Khushi Foundation is dedicated to the empowerment and welfare of all sections of the society-women, children, elderly and especially the physically & mentally challenged. Our aim is to empower the children having special needs with the necessary skills to manage life on their own. However, the thrust area of Khushi Foundation is empowerment of mentally and physically challenged children.

Its aim is to promote and conduct activities of health care, mass literacy, employment generation training activities, cultural and of charitable nature among the poor and the neglected brothers residing in undeveloped colonies and slums. Today we build capacities for them –social, vocational, emotional or physical - spread awareness and ensure that they get what is rightfully theirs. Today, when retirement is staring at many of our students, we are looking at innovative ways of making it an enjoyable phase of their lives.
